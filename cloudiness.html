Understanding Cloudiness for Farmers

Cloudiness affects sunlight and water needs for crops. Hereâ€™s a guide to help you make informed decisions.

Cloudiness Levels

  • 0% - 20%: Clear
  • 20% - 50%: Partly Cloudy
  • 50% - 80%: Mostly Cloudy
  • 80% - 100%: Overcast

What to Do Based on Cloudiness

0% - 20%: Clear

Water more; check crops regularly to prevent drying out.

20% - 50%: Partly Cloudy

Maintain your usual watering schedule; monitor soil moisture.

50% - 80%: Mostly Cloudy

Reduce watering; watch for rain and puddles in fields.

80% - 100%: Overcast

Pause watering if rain is expected; prevent waterlogging.
